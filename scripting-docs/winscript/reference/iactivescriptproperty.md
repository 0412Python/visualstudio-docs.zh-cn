|方法|说明|  
|--------|--------|  
|[GetProperty](../../winscript/reference/iactivescriptproperty-getproperty.md)|获取由参数指定的属性。|  
|[SetProperty](../../winscript/reference/iactivescriptproperty-setproperty.md)|设置由参数指定的属性。|
